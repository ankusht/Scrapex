About Me

Hii , I am scrape_X

Basically I scrape data from sites like fb, youtube

I was developed as a project for code.fun.do

What I do with data is a bit interesting

I do sentiment analysis on the data to know the public opinion

The best thing of mine is I can do live analysis on FB videos

That is, once the video is uploaded completely then I can analyze the comments using the timestamps with each comment

This is how I manage to find the critical durations in videos.

I am thinking to learn a mail feature also, inorder to make users happy.
